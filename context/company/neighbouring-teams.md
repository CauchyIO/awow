# Neighbouring teams (1° separation)

Teams whose work directly intersects ours. Per the blog (`input/PROPOSAL.md`'s anchor §3, §8), each team writes its own summary — these stubs are placeholders until the neighbouring teams fill them in.

`/setup-awow` Step 6 generates this file with empty stubs based on the 1° teams the user names.

## Template

### <Team name>

- **Mission:** <their one-line mission>
- **Members we work with:** <names>
- **Shared dependencies:** <APIs, repos, boards, data sources>
- **Cadence:** <how often we sync>
- **Board:** <URL>
- **Owner of this summary:** <name from the neighbouring team>

> This summary is owned by the neighbouring team. If it is stale, the fix is to ask them to update it, not to rewrite it ourselves.
